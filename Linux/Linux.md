Linux is often offered to the user in various _[Linux distributions](https://el.wikipedia.org/wiki/%CE%94%CE%B9%CE%B1%CE%BD%CE%BF%CE%BC%CE%AE_Linux "Linux distribution")_ . A characteristic of the distributions is the great possibility of customization and choice they offer as each one is addressed to a different type of user. Depending on the philosophy followed, each distribution may place greater emphasis on user-friendliness, multimedia applications, ease of customization, simplicity of the system, only free software, low resource requirements, and others.

The Linux kernel is the brainchild of [Linus Torvalds](https://el.wikipedia.org/wiki/%CE%9B%CE%AF%CE%BD%CE%BF%CF%85%CF%82_%CE%A4%CF%8C%CF%81%CE%B2%CE%B1%CE%BB%CE%BD%CF%84%CF%82 "Linus Torvalds") , from whom the name Linux is derived. Torvalds began developing an initial kernel in 1991 using code from [Andrew Tannenbaum](https://el.wikipedia.org/wiki/%CE%86%CE%BD%CF%84%CF%81%CE%B9%CE%BF%CF%85_%CE%A4%CE%B1%CE%BD%CE%B5%CE%BD%CE%BC%CF%80%CE%AC%CE%BF%CF%85%CE%BC "Άντριου Τανενμπάουμ") 's academic operating system [MINIX , which he independently developed and later adopted from](https://el.wikipedia.org/wiki/MINIX "MINIX") [Richard Stallman](https://el.wikipedia.org/wiki/%CE%A1%CE%AF%CF%84%CF%83%CE%B1%CF%81%CE%BD%CF%84_%CE%A3%CF%84%CF%8C%CE%BB%CE%BB%CE%BC%CE%B1%CE%BD "Ρίτσαρντ Στόλλμαν") 's [GNU](https://el.wikipedia.org/wiki/GNU "GNU") operating system programs and libraries . Torvalds' initial kernel has been worked on by thousands of users, communities, and companies. Because of the coexistence of the Linux kernel and the GNU system in the formation of Linux as an operating system, the system is often referred to as GNU/Linux, as preferred by the [Free Software Foundation](https://el.wikipedia.org/wiki/%CE%8A%CE%B4%CF%81%CF%85%CE%BC%CE%B1_%CE%95%CE%BB%CE%B5%CF%8D%CE%B8%CE%B5%CF%81%CE%BF%CF%85_%CE%9B%CE%BF%CE%B3%CE%B9%CF%83%CE%BC%CE%B9%CE%BA%CE%BF%CF%8D "Ίδρυμα Ελεύθερου Λογισμικού") . [[](https://el.wikipedia.org/wiki/Linux#cite_note-lsag-13) [13](https://el.wikipedia.org/wiki/Linux#cite_note-lsag-13) []](https://el.wikipedia.org/wiki/Linux#cite_note-lsag-13) [[](https://el.wikipedia.org/wiki/Linux#cite_note-gnu_homepage-14) [14](https://el.wikipedia.org/wiki/Linux#cite_note-gnu_homepage-14) []](https://el.wikipedia.org/wiki/Linux#cite_note-gnu_homepage-14)
